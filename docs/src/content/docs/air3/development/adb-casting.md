### Part 1 Troubleshooting Connection  
  
① Download the adb File & QtScrcpy-win-x64-v3.0.1 (2).zip: [adb.txt, QtScrcpy. Casting tool.](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1o-iD2WmcMKEPuZu9WhSYikpj1EVWy5gq?usp=sharing)  
  
  
![](public/images/air3/adb-for-casting-1.PNG)  
  
  
②Transfer the ADB file to your glasses' \[Internal Shared Storage] via USB.  
  
  
![](public/images/air3/adb-for-casting-2.PNG)  
  
  
### Part 2 Launch & Connect  
  
① Locate and open the "QtScrcpy-win-x64-v3.0.1" folder on your desktop.  
  
  
![](public/images/air3/adb-for-casting-3.PNG)  
  
  
② Double-click the QtScrcpy application icon to run the software.  
  
  
![](public/images/air3/adb-for-casting-4.PNG)  
  
  
③After entering the interface, click "One-Click USB Connection".  
  
  
![](public/images/air3/adb-for-casting-5.png)  
  
  
* After clicking, a search will be performed. Once the device is found, click again.  
  
  
![](public/images/air3/adb-for-casting-6.png)  
  
  
④The connection is successful. Access the glasses interface.  
  
  
![](public/images/air3/adb-for-casting-7.png)  
  
  
### Part 3 Troubleshooting Connection
  
Notes (If the device cannot be found, please adjust the following steps):  
  
  
① Restart the screen mirroring software:  
  
  
Completely close the screen mirroring application on your computer.  
  
  
Reopen the application and ensure it loads the new configuration.  
  
  
② Re-plug the USB data cable.

## 📦 What's Included

Inside your Air 3 box, you will find:

* Air Nose Pads & Cushions

* Three sizes of replacement nose pads: Small (S), Medium (M), and Large (L)

These accessories help adjust the height and angle of your glasses for optimal comfort and fit.





## 🔧 Step-by-Step Replacement Guide

### Step 1: Prepare Your Tools

* The included small screwdriver.

* The new nose pad and cushion you wish to install.

* A clean, well-lit workspace to avoid losing small parts.

### Step 2: Remove the Original Nose Pad

1. Gently pull down the rubber nose cushion to expose the tiny screw beneath.

2. Using the small screwdriver, turn the screw counterclockwise to loosen it.

   * *Tip: Be careful—the screw is very small and can be easily dropped.*

3. Once the screw is removed, you can take out the entire nose pad frame.

4. Set the old parts aside in a safe place.

### Step 3: Install the New Nose Pad

1. Align the new nose pad frame with the mounting hole on the glasses.

2. Insert the screw and use the screwdriver to turn it clockwise until snug.

   * *Do not overtighten—fasten just enough to secure the frame.*

![](public/images/air3/nose-pad.PNG)

3. Press the Air Nose Cushion firmly into place until you hear a soft click, indicating it is fully seated.

### Step 4: Fit and Adjust

* Put on your glasses and adjust the position for comfort.

* If needed, you can gently pull the cushion away to remove it and try a different size.

## 💡 Helpful Tips

* Always handle the screws and small parts over a tray or cloth.

* If switching between sizes, store unused nose pads in their original packaging.

* Ensure the cushion clicks into place to prevent it from coming loose during use.

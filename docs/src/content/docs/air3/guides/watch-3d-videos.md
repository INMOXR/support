## Step 1 **Transfer Video**

After connecting the glasses to the computer, find "IMA301" or "Internal Shared Storage" in "This PC" and drag the 3D video into the glasses folder.

![](public/images/air3/3d-videos-1.png)

![](public/images/air3/3d-videos-2.png)

## Step 2 **Open Gallery**

Click "Gallery" on the home page to open the album and view the video.

![](public/images/air3/3d-videos-3.png)

![](public/images/air3/3d-videos-4.png)

## Step 3 **Activate 3D Mode**

Open the video, and click the 3D icon in the upper right corner to enter the 3D cinema mode.

![](public/images/air3/3d-videos-5.png)
